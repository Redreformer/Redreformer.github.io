# Privacy Policy — My Work Diary

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview
My Work Diary is designed with your privacy in mind. All data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

## Data We Collect
We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information — including duties, holidays, and allowances — is saved locally on your device using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults and the app's Documents folder).

## Camera & Photo Library
The app requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to photograph and store your duty board. These photos are saved locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

## Siri & Shortcuts
The app integrates with Apple Shortcuts to allow you to query your duty times. No data is sent to third parties through this feature.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
