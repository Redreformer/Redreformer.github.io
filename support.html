Common Questions

How do I scan my duty board?

Tap the camera icon on the main screen. Point your camera at your duty board and tap the shutter button. The app will automatically detect your duty number and shift times.

Where is my data stored?

All your data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud. Your schedule is completely private.

How do I add a second profile?

Go to Settings and tap "Add Profile". You can switch between profiles from the main settings screen.
